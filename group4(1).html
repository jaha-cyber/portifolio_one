Current Health Insights

Vaccination Awareness

Staying up-to-date with immunizations is the most effective way to protect yourself and the community. Our clinics offer seasonal flu shots and essential childhood vaccines every Tuesday.

Nutrition for Families

Good health starts in the kitchen. Incorporating local vegetables and reducing processed sugars can significantly decrease the risk of lifestyle diseases like diabetes.

Mental Health Matters

Community well-being includes emotional health. We are now offering free counseling sessions for those dealing with stress or anxiety every Friday afternoon.

Physical Activity

Walking just 30 minutes a day can improve cardiovascular health. Join our local "Walking Warriors" group meeting at the community center every morning.
